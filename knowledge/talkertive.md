\# TALKERTIVE.IO



Last Updated: May 2026



\## Overview



Talkertive.io is an AI Voice Receptionist platform being developed under BusyAccess.



Its purpose is to help businesses answer calls, capture leads, schedule appointments, and provide customer service 24/7 without hiring additional staff.



\## Mission



Enable businesses to never miss an opportunity because a phone call went unanswered.



\## Product Vision



An AI-powered receptionist that:



\* Answers calls

\* Captures leads

\* Schedules appointments

\* Handles FAQs

\* Routes calls

\* Sends follow-up SMS messages

\* Operates 24/7
