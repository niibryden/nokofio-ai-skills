\# KASAKUA.IO



Last Updated: May 2026



\## Overview



Kasakua.io is an AI Customer Service Agent platform developed under BusyAccess.



Its purpose is to provide intelligent website chat, customer support automation, lead capture, appointment booking, and AI-powered customer engagement.



\## Mission



Help businesses automate customer interactions without sacrificing customer experience.



\## Product Vision



An AI agent that:



\- Answers customer questions

\- Captures leads

\- Books appointments

\- Qualifies prospects

\- Escalates support requests

\- Integrates with business systems
